Italian Nigga Meatballs

The Italian Meatballs for niggas

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups of Italian bread crumbs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1/4 medium onion finely diced or grated
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 1bsp fresh parsley
  • 1 cup of bbc junior
    •

    Recipe

    1. Preheat oven to 400F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a baking mat.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, add Italian bread crumbs and milk. Combine well and set aside while preparing the other Ingredients, for at least 5 mins.
    3. Add ground beef, onion, garlic, egg salt, black pepper, Parmesan cheese, and parsley to the bread crumb mixture, and mix with your hands until just combined. Do not overmix.
    4. Portion onto the prepared baking sheet(using a cookie or ice cream scoop will make it easier). Wet your hands with water, and roll meatballs into small golf ball size shapes
    5. bake for about 18-20 mins, or until the meatballs have reached an internal temp of 165F. Remove from oven and drain off any excess fat. Server with your favorite sauce and enjoy.

    Editors note only real niggas enjoy meat with their balls.
